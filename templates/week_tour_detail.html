Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Tirana

Morning: Airport pickup and transfer to hotel in Tirana

Afternoon: Tirana city walking tour - Skanderbeg Square, Et'hem Bey Mosque, Clock Tower, National History Museum

Evening: Welcome dinner at traditional Albanian restaurant

Accommodation: Hotel in Tirana center

Day 2: Tirana - Berat (UNESCO Site)

Morning: Visit Bunk'Art museum, then depart for Berat (2.5 hours drive)

Afternoon: Explore Berat Castle and Mangalem quarter - "City of a Thousand Windows"

Evening: Free time to explore Ottoman-era bazaar

Accommodation: Traditional hotel in Berat

Day 3: Berat - Apollonia - Gjirokaster

Morning: Visit ancient Apollonia archaeological park

Afternoon: Drive to Gjirokaster (2 hours), explore the Castle and old bazaar

Evening: Traditional dinner with folk music performance

Accommodation: Stone house hotel in Gjirokaster

Day 4: Gjirokaster - Butrint - Blue Eye - Saranda

Morning: Visit Butrint National Park (UNESCO World Heritage site)

Afternoon: Marvel at the Blue Eye natural spring phenomenon

Evening: Arrive in Saranda, free time at the seaside promenade

Accommodation: Beachfront hotel in Saranda

Day 5: Albanian Riviera - Ksamil - Llogara Pass

Morning: Boat trip to Ksamil islands - pristine beaches and crystal waters

Afternoon: Drive through Llogara National Park with spectacular mountain views

Evening: Sunset dinner overlooking the Ionian Sea

Accommodation: Mountain lodge near Llogara Pass

Day 6: Return to Tirana - Departure

Morning: Scenic drive back to Tirana (4 hours) with stops for photos

Afternoon: Last-minute shopping at Tirana's Grand Park area

Evening: Airport transfer for departure or hotel drop-off for extended stay
